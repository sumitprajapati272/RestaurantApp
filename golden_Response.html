Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Served with Soul

Ember & Oak began as a dream — to honour India's rich culinary tapestry while weaving in global techniques. Every dish tells a story of provenance: sourced from local farms, aged in our cellars, and kissed by fire in our custom wood-burning hearths.

We believe great dining is theatre. From the moment you enter, every sense is engaged — the warm amber light, the gentle crackle of oak, the aromas that greet you like an old friend.
